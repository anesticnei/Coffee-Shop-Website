About Lurr

Welcome to our coffee shop restaurant in the beautiful city of Kraków! We have been serving our customers since 2018 and are passionate about providing the best coffee and cakes to our community.

At our coffee shop, we believe in creating an inviting and cozy atmosphere where our customers can relax and enjoy their favorite coffee blends, along with our freshly baked cakes and pastries. We carefully select and roast our coffee beans to ensure that every cup of coffee we serve is rich in flavor and aroma.
