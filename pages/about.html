About Lurr

Welcome to our coffee shop restaurant in the beautiful city of Kraków! We have been serving our customers since 2018 and are passionate about providing the best coffee and cakes to our community.

At our coffee shop, we believe in creating an inviting and cozy atmosphere where our customers can relax and enjoy their favorite coffee blends, along with our freshly baked cakes and pastries. We carefully select and roast our coffee beans to ensure that every cup of coffee we serve is rich in flavor and aroma.

Our menu features a wide variety of coffee drinks, from classic espresso to specialty lattes, along with a selection of teas, hot chocolate, and refreshing smoothies. We also take pride in our selection of homemade cakes, pastries, and sandwiches, made fresh every day using high-quality ingredients.

We are committed to providing excellent service and creating a warm and welcoming environment for our customers. Whether you are looking for a quiet place to work or catch up with friends, our coffee shop is the perfect spot.

Thank you for choosing our coffee shop restaurant in Kraków. We look forward to serving you soon!
