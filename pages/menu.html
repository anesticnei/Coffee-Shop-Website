COFFEE DRINKS:

Espresso: A classic shot of rich, bold coffee.

Americano: Espresso shots topped with hot water, for a milder, smoother flavor.

Cappuccino: Espresso with steamed milk and a thick layer of froth.

Latte: Espresso with steamed milk, topped with a thin layer of froth.

Mocha: Espresso with steamed milk and chocolate syrup, topped with whipped cream.

Cold Brew: A refreshing, smooth coffee drink brewed with cold water.

BAKED GOODS:

Cakes: We offer a variety of cakes, from classic chocolate cake to fruity cheesecakes.

Pastries: Our selection of pastries includes croissants, muffins, and danishes, all made fresh daily.

Cookies: Our cookies come in a variety of flavors, including chocolate chip, oatmeal raisin, and peanut butter.

OTHER TREATS:

Hot Chocolate: Rich and creamy, our hot chocolate is the perfect treat on a cold day.

Tea: We offer a selection of premium teas, including herbal and fruit blends.

Smoothies: Our smoothies are made with fresh fruit and yogurt, and come in a variety of flavors.
