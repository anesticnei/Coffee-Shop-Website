Events

At our coffee shop, we don't just serve great coffee and food - we also offer a beautiful and unique space for events! Whether you're planning a birthday party, business meeting, baby shower, or any other special occasion, we have the perfect venue for you.

Our event space is versatile and can be customized to meet your needs. We offer a range of catering options, from coffee and cakes to full meals, all prepared with the same care and attention to detail as our regular menu items. Our experienced staff is here to ensure that your event runs smoothly and that your guests have an unforgettable experience.

In addition to our beautiful event space, we also offer off-site catering services. We can provide delicious food and beverages for your event, whether it's at your home, office, or another venue of your choice.

So, if you're looking for a unique and memorable venue for your next event, look no further than our coffee shop restaurant in Kraków. Contact us today to learn more about our event services and to start planning your special occasion with us!
